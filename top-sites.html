EXPLORE OMAN'S TREASURES

MUSCAT INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT

Muscat International Airport is the Sultanate of Oman's primary gateway and one of the most modern airports in the region. It combines modern designs and integrated services to provide a comfortable and safe travel experience for passengers...

THE OMAN CONVENTION AND EXHIBITION CENTRE

The Oman Convention and Exhibition Centre in Muscat is one of the region's leading destinations for hosting international events, exhibitions, and conferences. Featuring a modern architectural design and a strategic location...

MUTTRAH SOUQ

Muttrah Souq in Muscat Governorate is one of the oldest and most famous traditional markets in the Sultanate of Oman and a prominent tourist destination that reflects the fragrance of history and the spirit of Omani authenticity...

THE SULTAN QABOOS GRAND MOSQUE

The Sultan Qaboos Grand Mosque in Muscat is one of the most important religious and architectural landmarks in the Sultanate of Oman, a symbol of luxury and artistic creativity. Opened in 2001 by order of the late Sultan Qaboos bin Said...

THE ROYAL OPERA HOUSE MUSCAT

The Royal Opera House Muscat is a prominent architectural and cultural masterpiece in the Sultanate of Oman, a center for fine arts and world music. Opened in 2011 by order of the late Sultan Qaboos bin Said...

THE NATIONAL MUSEUM

The National Museum in Muscat is the most prominent cultural edifice that tells the story of Oman throughout the ages. Opened in 2016, it serves as a platform for preserving and displaying Omani heritage in a modern, sophisticated style...
